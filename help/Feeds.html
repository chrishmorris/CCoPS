News Feeds in PLan

PLan will provide some news feeds, so you can keep up to date with progress in shared projects. It already provides links to some public news feeds of use to protein scientists.

Getting a News Feed

Available news feeds.

Subscribing to a News Feed

In Outlook 2010: right click on the feed icon and copy the link address. Open Outlook and right click on the folder "RSS Feeds", and click on "Add a New RSS Feed". Paste in the URL of the feed and click "Add".

In Chrome: install the RSS Feed Reader extension, and restart Chrome. Once you have that extension, click on the feed icon to view it. (At the time of writing, Chrome sometimes fails to provide a user friendly view of the feed.) Click on the RSS Feed Reader icon, then click on the "+" button, then on the "+ Follow" button.

In Firefox: click on the feed icon to view it. Then click on the "Subscribe Now" button.

In Internet Explorer: click on the feed icon to view it. Then click on "Subscribe to this feed". If the page does not include this button, then first you need to turn on feed reading view and restart the browser. This is in Internet Options, in Content tab, in the settings for "Feeds and Web Slices".

On a mobile device: install a feed reader, e.g. feedly.

Types of News Feed

PLan provides feeds according to the "Atom" standard. Plan also provides links to feeds that conform to one of the "RSS" standards.
