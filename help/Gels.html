Annotating a Gel in PLan

You can load a gel image into PLan, outline tracks, type a description for each track, and save the annotated image.

Loading a Gel

Visit the gel annotator. Drag an image from a folder and drop it on the page, or click the "Browse..." button and choose the file.

Outlining a Track

If you have a touch screen, you can select a track with your finger, otherwise use a mouse. Start at one corner of the area of interest, and drag to the opposite corner. After you release the mouse or lift your finger, type the information about the track you have outlined.

To remove an outline, and the associated note, click "Delete".

Saving

Click "". The file will be saved as a .eml file. You can open this file in your email program, or in MS Word.

Future developments

Later PLan will include a file store where you can back up your gels.
