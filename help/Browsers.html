Browsers for PLan

PLan is tested in current Chrome, Firefox, and in Internet Explorer 10. Please get in touch if you have problems on any of these platforms.

Making a shortcut to PLan

First visit your favourite page in PLan, and bookmark it. Then drag the bookmark to the desktop.

Your network connection

You can install a copy of PLan as a local web app. Parts of PLan work even if you have no network connection. Other parts require you to be online.
